In this calculator, you can add many fixed items and their weekly, monthly, or yearly costs. The total daily, weekly, and monthly fixed costs will be computed. Both your inputs and results are saved for you to reference and update in the future. Below is a list of common fixed cost items.

  • Truck Payment
  • Trailer Payment
  • Tire Cost
  • Maintenance
  • Permits
  • Health Insurance
  • Disability Insurance
  • Miscellaneous
  • Food
  • Lodging
  • Phone
  • Internet
