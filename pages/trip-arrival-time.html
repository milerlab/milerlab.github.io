This is a trip ETA estimator. It also estimates the trip start time when a required arrival time is entered. If your trip has multiple stops, you can click the + button to add a stop. Distance = Drive Hours times Distance per Hour (MPH or KPH). All hours fields are in HH:mm format (e.g. 09:15 or 21:30).

  • d:HH:mm: d (days): trip days (1 to 9). HH (hours): 0 to 23. mm (minutes): 0 to 59
  • Distance per Hour: miles per hour or kilometers per hour
  • Start Time: trip start time or previous stop departure time in d:HH:mm
  • Distance to Stop: miles or kilometers to next stop
  • Drive Hours to Stop: hours of driving needed to reach next stop
  • Idle Hours to Stop: hours of non-driving activities needed to reach next stop, e.g., rest, break, meal break and fuel stop
  • Arrival Time: stop arrival time in d:HH:mm
  • Window Start: (optional) If you arrive before the window start time, you must wait (in d:HH:mm)
  • Window End: (optional) Arrival after window end time is considered being late (in d:HH:mm)
  • Hours Wait: Window Start Time minus Arrival Time (for early arrival only)
  • Hours Late: Arrival Time minus Window End Time
  • Load/Unload Hours: load/unload hours at the stop
  • Departure Time: stop departure time in d:HH:mm
  • Hours Between Stops: elapsed time from start time to stop departure
