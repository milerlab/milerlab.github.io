This computes the markup percent on the product cost, the final sale or selling price, and the total margin. For example, you can enter the original cost and your required margin to calculate the selling price and markup percent. Since this is an all-directional calculator, just input the fields you know and tap CALCULATE.

If you would like to see your margin per unit, please check the Single Unit checkbox. Break-Even Point is the minimum number of units to sell so that total sale is not less than total cost.

  • Cost Price: the cost of one product unit
  • Sell Price: the sell price of one product unit
  • Markup: the sell price minus the cost price
  • Markup Percent (%): (the markup amount divided by the cost price) times 100
  • Sell Units: the number of units for sale
  • Fixed Cost: the amount spent that is not dependent on the product units
  • Total Cost: number of units times the cost price, plus the fixed cost
  • Total Sale: number of units times the sell price
  • Total Margin: total sales minus total cost
  • Total Margin Percent (%): (total margin divided by total sales) times 100
  • Break-Even Point: It will be shown in the Units field, when you set Margin to 0.0 and input other needed fields like cost and sell price.
